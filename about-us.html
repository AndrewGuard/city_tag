About Us

My City Tag LLC was founded in May 2013 by two former Motorola executives, Steve Bell and Sanjay Gupta.

Both founders have significant experience creating innovative and technologically sophisticated products for business and consumer markets. Both are seasoned international business professionals who have developed and grown new and existing global business operations.

The founders’ initial focus was developing a simple mobile application to fill a void in the Chicago parking experience. While exploring and researching the U.S. and global parking market and applying creative systems thinking, it was apparent there was a bigger issue. In defining the solution, it became clear to the team that they had developed a unique and patentable invention that can be applied to the convergence of emerging Internet of Things and mobile internet markets, and the resulting big data opportunity.

Meet our Team

Steve Bell

Steve Bell is an accomplished strategy, marketing and technology consultant, analyst, speaker and commentator on the mobile Internet and its impact on digital life. Steve worked with Motorola for over 20 years in Germany, the UK and USA, and has an in-depth understanding of international trends, markets and cultures. He is an international advisor to the ICIN Conference and the Center for Policy on Emerging Technologies (C-PET).

Sanjay Gupta

Sanjay Gupta, Ph.D., is an innovator and entrepreneurial executive and has held numerous leadership positions in consumer devices and wireless communications industry for over 15 years. Most recently he was VP of Engineering at Motorola Mobility and led Product Management and Development for the companion Products business. Sanjay also served as a Professor in the Dept. of EE/CE at Illinois Institute of Technology

Our Inspiration

Motivation

The founders, frustrated by their parking experience in their beloved city of Chicago, decided to bring their innovative problem-solving skills to create a no hassle parking experience. This led to the creation of the patent pending My City Tag, a personal parking meter.

Platform

The founders soon realized that the platform they had created transcended parking and can be applied to simplify and enhance multiple city experiences, and they are working to realize xxx the “Smart Digital City” vision.
